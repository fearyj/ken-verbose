## Welcome

This is my first blog post on **ken --verbose**. I built this site as a place to share my projects, document what I'm learning, and keep a public log of my journey as a developer.

## What to Expect

I plan to write about:

- **Projects** — deep dives into things I'm building
- **Tutorials** — walkthroughs of interesting problems I've solved
- **Thoughts** — opinions on tools, workflows, and the dev ecosystem

## Why "verbose"?

In most CLI tools, the `--verbose` flag gives you extra detail. That's what this blog is — the extended output of what I'm working on. Instead of just shipping code, I want to explain the *why* behind decisions and share lessons learned.
